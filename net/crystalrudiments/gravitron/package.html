At the start of the program, there is nothing but space. The user can create planets with specific trajectories, sizes, and velocities by clicking and dragging with the mouse. The left mouse key and right mouse key produce different sized planets. By selecting a planet, it becomes the center of view, so that it always stays in the exact center of the screen.

The program has options for saving, loading, and starting a new session. All of the internal components of the program are, of course, reusable, so you can adapt them for your application.
